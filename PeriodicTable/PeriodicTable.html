Hydrogen

Hydrogen is the lightest and most abundant element in the universe, constituting roughly 75% of all baryonic mass.

Properties:

Atomic Number: 1

Atomic Mass: 1.008 g/mol

Phase at Room Temperature: Gas

Melting Point: -259.14°C

Boiling Point: -252.87°C

Density: 0.08988 g/L

Electronegativity: 2.20 (Pauling scale)
